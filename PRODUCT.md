# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Croatian general public: people wanting to nominate needy families (elderly, families in difficult situations), potential donors, volunteers and tradespeople willing to contribute time and skills. Secondary: families seeking help. Users typically arrive via social media or word of mouth, motivated by community spirit and the desire to do something concrete.

## Product Purpose
Instalater Anđeo (Angel Plumber) is a humanitarian project by Petrokov d.o.o. that renovates bathrooms for individuals and families in greatest need — at no cost. The landing page exists to collect candidate nominations, attract donors and volunteer tradespeople, and communicate credibility. Success = a completed nomination or donation intent.

## Brand Personality
Toplo, pouzdano, zajedničarsko (warm, trustworthy, communal). The brand speaks like a neighbor who genuinely cares — not a charity that creates distance between giver and receiver. Voice: direct, emotional, grounded. Avoids bureaucratic NGO tone and performative charity marketing.

## Anti-references
Cold corporate NGO sites with stock photos of suffering. Government social services portals (clinical, distant). Cheap telethon aesthetics with countdown clocks and guilt-trip copy. Over-designed agency portfolios that prioritize style over warmth.

## Design Principles
1. **Dostojanstvo iznad sažaljenja** — Frame help as restoring dignity, not pity. Copy and imagery make the recipient feel seen, not exposed.
2. **Zajednica, ne institucija** — Tone is neighborhood, not organization. People helping people, not a bureaucracy distributing aid.
3. **Povjerenje kroz konkretnost** — Trust is built with specifics: partner names, real renovation photos, exact mission statements. No vague impact claims.
4. **Toplina u svakom detalju** — Warmth permeates typography, spacing, color, micro-copy. Never cold or clinical.
5. **Akcija nad pasivnošću** — Every section should have a clear path forward: nominate, donate, volunteer.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA target. Skip-to-content link present. Aria-labels on interactive and media elements. Croatian primary language, hr-HR locale. Animations via CSS transitions — RevealOnScroll should respect prefers-reduced-motion.
